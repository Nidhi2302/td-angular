ABOUT US

When great people come together, amazing things happen. Need to Park, “Truck Parking Easy” is the result of two creative problem solvers and the input from a team of long haul drivers that were willing to share their experiences and frustrations in hopes that they would be listened to… and that a resolution could be found. 

Israel Almanzar, a long-haul driver, approached two investors with an idea he had as a result of many conversations he had with fellow drivers about the difficulty of finding places to park where and when they needed it. 

Those creative investors Joe Wojciechowski and Bill McAdory listened to  what the long haul drivers were struggling with. They then took a step back and looked at what options were currently available, researched the major corridors and the pain points that drivers faced every day realizing a a driver co-op was absent. That is, a place that allowed Over the Road (OTR) drivers to connect and help each other find safe places to park. 

That is how Need to Park, “Truck Parking Made Easy” came into existence. His idea turned into the exciting app that makes finding parking easy and provides a way for OTR drivers to work together.  What makes us different is our ability to connect long haul drivers with one another to find available parking along their routes.  Departing drivers are able to post when they are leaving a spot, and when another driver needs the spot around the same departure time, those two drivers make the agreement for the spot. Driver to driver, we are cut out the middle person. 

Most OTR drivers know of all the common truck stops, and understand the struggle of trying to get there by a certain time to secure a spot. What Need to Park wanted to do was to create a way for you to find parking when the driver actually needs it. A safe parking spot that fits with the driver’s schedule, not the schedule and availability of a truck stop. 

Need to Park, “Truck Parking Made Easy” aims to be a trusted source among the dedicated OTR drivers. We not only want to provide solutions for long haul drivers that not only work, we also want to provide what drivers actually need and when they need it.
